Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Focus Mate ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use the Focus Mate application ("App").

By using Focus Mate, you agree to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect name, email address, and password (stored securely) through Firebase Authentication. You may also upload a profile picture and username.
  • Task & Goal Data: We store your tasks, notes, goals, and related metadata.
  • Device Info: Anonymous data about your device, OS, and app usage is collected for performance monitoring.
  • Voice Input: Used with our AI assistant and only processed temporarily — not permanently stored.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core features like authentication and task saving
  • To personalize your experience
  • To analyze usage and improve app performance
  • To enable voice assistant functionality
  • To keep your data available offline when needed

3. Data Security

We use secure technologies like HTTPS, Firebase Authentication, and Firestore encryption to protect your data. All profile images are securely uploaded to Supabase.

4. Guest Mode

You can use Focus Mate in Guest Mode without signing in. In this mode, your data remains local to your device and is not synced to the cloud.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Trusted services like Firebase and Supabase, under their privacy terms
  • Authorities if required for legal compliance

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

To delete your account, email us at support@focusmate-app.com. We’ll permanently delete your data within 7 business days.

7. Your Rights

  • You can view and update your profile information
  • You can request to delete your account and all stored data
  • You can request a copy of your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. If there are major changes, we’ll notify you via email or an in-app message.
