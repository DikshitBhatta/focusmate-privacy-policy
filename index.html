Privacy Policy for Focus Mate

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Focus Mate (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about users of the Focus Mate app.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Account Information: Name, email address, and password (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Optional Info: Profile image, phone number.
  • Task & Goal Data: Tasks, notes, goals, and preferences saved by the user.
  • Device Info: App usage logs, device type, and OS version (anonymized).
  • Voice Input: Transcriptions of AI assistant requests (processed but not stored permanently).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To sync your tasks, goals, and settings across devices.
  • To improve functionality and performance of the app.

3. Data Security

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore to securely store data. All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

4. Guest Mode

Guest mode allows you to use the app without an account. Your data is stored locally and not uploaded to our servers.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except for essential services like:

  • Firebase (authentication, database)
  • Google APIs (voice-to-text services)

6. Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account or data, please contact us at support@focusmate-app.com. Requests are handled within 7 business days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Major changes will be communicated via app notifications or email (if provided).

If you have questions, contact us at support@focusmate-app.com.
