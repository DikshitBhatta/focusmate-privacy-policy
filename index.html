Focus Mate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Focus Mate ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use the Focus Mate application ("App").

By using Focus Mate, you agree to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our service to you. The types of data we collect include:

  • Personal Information:
    • Account Information: When you sign up or log in via Firebase Authentication, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored securely).
    • Profile Information: You may choose to update your profile with additional details like a profile image and username.
    • Optional Information: Users may also provide a phone number (optional).
  • Task & Goal Data:
    • We store tasks, goals, notes, and their associated data for use within the app.
  • Device Information:
    • We collect anonymous device information including your device type, operating system, and app usage statistics for app performance improvement and crash analytics.
  • Voice Input:
    • If you use the AI Assistant feature, we may collect your voice input, which is transcribed into text using speech-to-text services. This data is used only for processing your queries and is not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain the App, including user account management and authentication.
  • To personalize your experience, such as saving your tasks, notes, and goals.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the performance and functionality of the App.
  • To provide you with responses and suggestions through the AI Assistant feature.
  • To send you updates, including new features, bug fixes, and security patches.
  • To provide a smooth user experience even in offline mode, by syncing tasks, notes, and progress.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Specifically:

  • Encrypted Data: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Data Storage: Personal data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore and image data in Supabase.
  • Authentication: Your account is authenticated using Firebase Authentication, which uses secure methods to protect your login credentials.

4. Guest Mode

You can use Focus Mate in Guest Mode without creating an account. In Guest Mode, your data is not saved to the cloud, and tasks or notes are stored only locally on your device. You may later choose to create an account and sync your data to the cloud.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party service providers like Firebase (for authentication and database) and Supabase (for image storage), which have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Focus Mate, its users, or others.

6. Permissions and Consent

We request permissions at runtime for sensitive features (microphone). Before any such data is accessed, we will show a clear explanation and ask for your consent.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with the services. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by following the steps below:

  • Delete Account: To delete your account, please contact us at Yummin.shin@gmail.com. All personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access Your Data: You can access and update your profile information at any time within the app.
  • Request Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or through a notification within the App.
